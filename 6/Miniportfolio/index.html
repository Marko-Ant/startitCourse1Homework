Elementarium - a board game for 1-4 players

An interactive spin on an otherwise more solitare concept - in Elementarium, players try to bluff their way into collecting cards from the 4 elemental decks, and slowly build their resource engine, which is dependant on collecting resources the player to your right is spending. Best played with 4. Games can last up to two hours.
