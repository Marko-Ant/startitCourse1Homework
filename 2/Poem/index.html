A Shy Little Poem

This is a poem, but it's a little shy, you know
I guess you already figured out how to make it show

But I wouldn't even presume to call it a real poem
In fact, we are barely past the first verse, and it doesn't even rhyme anymore

Although poems are just words, and we think they are better words than other words
Yes, this one is definitely just a lot of words.

I lied, it's not just words, that last one was a picture
at least it should be, if my html/css skills didn't fail me



And if you were wondering - that last one was blank on purpose
Because nothing is perfect, and definitely not this little poem

And I guess it has to end somewhere
After all, I'm out of ideas, and out of knowledge...

...and out of time to google everything I don't know,
So I bid you farewell, and thank you for staying, until the end, with this little poem.
